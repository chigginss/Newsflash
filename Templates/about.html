Newsflash displays top trending content in a D3.js visual.
It provides context about media outlets, organizing trending stories by the popularity and bias of the respective media outlet.
Newsflash was created by Cierra Higgins for her HackBright Academy final project.

How Newsflash Works

Trending Coverage - NewsAPI

All news covearge comes from NewsAPI.
NewsAPI decides internally which articles are Trending using their 'Top Trending' endpoint.
Please visit the NewsAPI site for a complete list of outlets NewsAPI uses for Top Trending coverage.

Bias and Popularity Metrics

Media Bias and Popularity ratings are not added from NewsAPI. Cierra researched and collected these metrics for each media outlet.
All Bias ratings come from AllSides
All popularity metrics are based off of Amazon's Alexa's Top 500 Websites, filtered by news and media.
If you disagree with the rating of an outlet's popularity or bias, or do not think that an outlet should be included, please send feedback here.
