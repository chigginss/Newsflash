Newsflash displays the most popular news breaking right now in a D3.js visual.
It organizes trending stories by the popularity and bias of the respective media outlet.
Newsflash was created by Cierra Higgins for her HackBright Academy final project.

Trending Coverage - NewsAPI

All news coverage comes from NewsAPI.
NewsAPI decides internally which articles are Trending using their 'Top Trending' endpoint.
Please visit the NewsAPI site for a complete list of outlets NewsAPI uses for Top Trending coverage.

Bias and Popularity Metrics

All Bias ratings come from AllSides.
All popularity metrics are based off of Amazon Alexa's Top 500 Websites, filtered by news and media.
If you disagree with the rating of an outlet's popularity or bias, please provide feedback here.
